Japanese Riichi Mahjong Guide - v1.0 (2026)

A step-by-step guide for beginners to learn and play Japanese Riichi Mahjong

About This Guide

This resource explains tiles, gameplay rules, yaku, scoring, and includes practice exercises in a clear, approachable way. The guide contains printable learning packets designed to teach Riichi Mahjong step-by-step.
